Commission

Welcome! I offer bespoke music compositions for a wide variety of projects. Whether you need orchestral works, chamber pieces, or electronic scores, I can bring your vision to life.

Commissioning a Composition

Commissions are personalized based on your project’s scope and requirements. Whether you are looking for a short solo piece, a chamber work, or a full orchestral composition, I can create something tailored specifically to your needs.

If you are interested in commissioning a piece, please reach out to discuss the details of your project, and I will provide you with a customized quote.

Printing and Shipping (for Scores & Parts)

Printed scores and parts are available through a print-to-order service:

  • Full Score (200 pages): Available upon request (pricing varies depending on the order)
  • Parts (set of parts): Additional fees for printing and shipping may apply, typically around $100–$150 depending on the number of parts.

Shipping costs vary based on location and order size.

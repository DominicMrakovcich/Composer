Commission a Custom Composition

Welcome! I offer bespoke music compositions for a wide variety of projects. Whether you need orchestral works, chamber pieces, or electronic scores, I can bring your vision to life.

Pricing for Compositions

My rates are straightforward and based on the length of the music. Here’s how it breaks down:

Composition Rates

$1,000 per minute

This includes the composition, orchestration, and full score preparation. Revisions can be discussed depending on the complexity and amount of work involved.

For example:

  • 5-minute composition: $5,000
  • 10-minute composition: $10,000
  • 45-minute composition: $45,000
  • 60-minute composition: $60,000

Printing and Shipping (for Scores & Parts)

Printed scores and parts are available through a print-to-order service. Below are the typical costs for printed versions:

  • Full Score (200 pages): $200 (plus printing & shipping fees)
  • Parts (set of parts): Additional fees for printing and shipping may apply, typically around $100-$150 depending on the number of parts.

Shipping costs vary depending on your location and the size of the order. Please contact me for an exact quote on shipping.

About

Dominic Mrakovcich is a composer and sound designer based in Marquette, Michigan. Inspired by his Brazilian and Croatian heritage, he blends traditional music with electronic and ambient sounds.

He studied percussion and film scoring, earning a Bachelor’s in Percussion Performance from UMass Amherst and a Master’s in Music Composition. His music ranges from orchestral pieces to experimental sound design, creating immersive and emotional experiences.

As Sound & Media Supervisor at Northern Michigan University, Dominic works with technology and music to craft unique audio experiences. His recent project, Alice in Wonderland: An Immersive Experience, uses spatial sound and electronic elements to bring stories to life. He also hosts Soundtrack Sundays, a radio show about film scores and storytelling through music.

Dominic has composed for films, theater, and multimedia projects, collaborating with musicians, directors, and artists. His work in the ambient music scene and projects like Hearding Dogs showcase his ability to create rich, atmospheric soundscapes.
